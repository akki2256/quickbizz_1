Introductory (Honeymoon) Rates

An introductory rate (also known as a “honeymoon rate”) is a promotional interest rate offered to new customers for a limited period. This rate typically applies for an initial period (commonly up to 12 months), after which the interest rate will revert to the lender’s standard variable rate in effect at that time. Customers should consider the ongoing interest rate when assessing the suitability of a loan product.
